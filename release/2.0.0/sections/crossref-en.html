Agentc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/hto#Agent

An Agent represents a historical individual or collective entity that plays an active or attributable role in relation to historical documents. Agents may be persons, organisations, institutions, or communities involved in the creation, production, dissemination, curation, or use of historical texts. They can also appear within texts as referenced or represented actors, such as authors, editors, correspondents, or subjects.
Example 
hto:dugald a hto:Agent, schema:Person;
  foaf:name "Dugald Stewart"^^xsd:string;
  hto:birthYear "1753"^^xsd:integer;
  hto:deathYear "1828"^^xsd:integer;
  hto:termsOfAddress "Prof."^^xsd:string.

hto:edition3 a hto:Edition, schema:CreativeWork;
  hto:title "Encyclopaedia Britannica";
  hto:number "3"^^xsd:integer;
  schema:editor hto:dugald.

AuthorityTypec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/hto#AuthorityType

AuthorityType represents a controlled set of types used to classify resources according to the authority or knowledge base they are associated with. Instances of this class indicate whether a resource corresponds to, for example, a Wikidata item, DBpedia resource, GeoNames feature, or other authority record. hto:AuthorityType is intended to be used with hto:hasAuthorityType on entities linked to external identifiers, without introducing separate classes for each authority source.
Example 
<https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q203000> a hto:ExternalRecord;
  hto:hasAuthorityType hto:WikidataItem.

hto:perth_scotland a skos:Concept, hto:Location;
  rdfs:label "Perth"^^xsd:integer;
  hto:hasConceptRecord <https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q203000>.
has super-classes
E55 Type c
is in range of
hasAuthorityType op
has members
DBpediaItem ni, WikidataItem ni

ConceptRecordc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/hto#ConceptRecord

A Concept Record is a specific record in a historical document or a modern work that descirbe a skos:concept. One concept might have multiple concept records. It is used to link records about the same concept across time and sources.

TermRecordc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/hto#TermRecord

A TermRecord represents an article or entry in a dictionary-like textual work that provides a description, definition, or explanation of a specific term. It applies to structured reference works such as encyclopaedias, dictionaries, lexicons, or descriptive gazetteers, where each entry corresponds to a named concept—e.g., a place, person, event, or general topic. A hto:TermRecord captures the conceptual focus of the entry (the term), its associated textual content, and any metadata or provenance relating to its creation, publication, or digitisation.

TextSegmentc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/hto#TextSegment

A TextSegment represents a specific segment of text within an hto:OriginalDescription. It is used to identify and describe spans such as persons, locations, events, dates, or other textual fragments produced by NER or other NLP tasks. As a subclass of oa:SpecificResource, a hto:TextSegment points to its source text and is refined by a selector (e.g. character offsets, quoted text). Annotations can then attach identified entities or NLP results as bodies (e.g. persons, places).
Example 
hto:OriginalDescription_Perth_1823 a hto:OriginalDescription ;
  hto:title "Perth" ;
  hto:text "Perth, a county in Scotland ..."^^xsd:string; .

hto:Segment_Scotland_001 a hto:TextSegment ;
  oa:hasSource hto:OriginalDescription_Perth_1842 ;
  oa:hasSelector [
    a oa:TextPositionSelector ;
    oa:start 19 ;
    oa:end   27
  ] .

hto:Annotation_Scotland_001 a oa:Annotation ;
  oa:hasTarget hto:Segment_Scotland_001 ;
  oa:hasBody hto:Location_Scotland.

hto:Location_Scotland a crm:E53_Place.
has super-classes
Specific Resource c
is in domain of
hasAnnotation op
